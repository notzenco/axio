# Code of conduct

Axio's community should be welcoming, technically rigorous, and safe to
participate in.

Be respectful, critique ideas rather than people, assume good faith while
still asking for evidence, and avoid harassment, discrimination, threats, or
the publication of private information. Maintainers may edit or remove
contributions and restrict participation when behavior undermines the project
or its community.

Report conduct concerns privately to the repository owner. Good-faith reports
will be handled as confidentially as practical.
